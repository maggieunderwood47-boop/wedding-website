The Beginning

A Hinge match, and then some.

It started the old fashioned way — a match on Hinge that turned into an actual conversation, and then an actual date. What kept it going was how easily everything else lined up: a shared love of outdoor adventures, a similar taste in music, and the kind of active lifestyle where "let's do something this weekend" was never a hard sell.

Somewhere along the way, our friend groups met and got along just as well as we did — which made everything feel less like blending two lives and more like they were already headed in the same direction. We share the values that matter most, with just enough differences to keep things interesting. We think that's the makings of a perfect bond.
